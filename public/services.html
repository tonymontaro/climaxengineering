About Us

Climax Engineering Limited is an indigenous Oil and gas services Company. It was incorporated in 2013 with the aim of carrying out oil and gas services in the Nigerian oil and gas industry. We have assembled an experienced board and management team with established and successful track records in acquiring and developing oil and gas interests.

Our Services

Procurement in the Oil and Gas industry has become increasingly demanding in today's competitive marketplace. At Climax Engineering, we pride ourselves in our expertise to source for services and supplies from all corners of the world. We supply all the manufactured equipment associated with the exploration and delivery of oil and gas from Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs).
