About Us

Climax Engineering Limited is an indigenous Oil and gas services Company. It was incorporated in 2013 with the aim of carrying out oil and gas services in the Nigerian oil and gas industry. We have assembled an experienced board and management team with established and successful track records in acquiring and developing oil and gas interests.
